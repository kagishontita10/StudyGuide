IT Specializations

Software Development

Focuses on designing, coding, testing, and maintaining software applications across platforms.

Career Paths: Software engineer, app developer, backend programmer, DevOps specialist.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Mathematics, Computer Studies, English
  • Minimum APS and logical reasoning
  • Strong problem-solving and coding skills

Cybersecurity

Protects systems and data from digital threats through encryption, monitoring, and ethical hacking.

Career Paths: Security analyst, penetration tester, risk assessor, compliance officer.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Mathematics, Computer Studies, English
  • Minimum APS and logical reasoning
  • Strong problem-solving and coding skills

Data Science

Uses statistics, machine learning, and programming to extract insights from complex datasets.

Career Paths: Data analyst, machine learning engineer, business intelligence developer.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Mathematics, Computer Studies, English
  • Minimum APS and logical reasoning
  • Strong problem-solving and coding skills

General Entry Requirements

To study Information Technology, students typically need:

  • Strong performance in Mathematics and Computer Studies
  • Logical thinking and technical curiosity
  • Ability to work independently and in teams
