Faculty of Medicine

Dedicated to healing, research, and advancing human health.

Why Study Medicine?

Medicine is a noble profession focused on diagnosing, treating, and preventing illness. It combines scientific knowledge with compassionate care to improve lives.

Medical Disciplines

General Medicine

Focuses on internal medicine, diagnostics, and patient care across all age groups.

Career Paths: General practitioner, hospitalist, medical researcher.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Mathematics and English proficiency
  • Minimum APS and entrance exam

Surgery

Specializes in operative procedures to treat injuries, diseases, and deformities.

Career Paths: Surgeon, trauma specialist, surgical consultant.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Mathematics and English proficiency
  • Minimum APS and entrance exam

Public Health

Focuses on population health, disease prevention, and health policy.

Career Paths: Epidemiologist, health educator, policy advisor.

Key Subjects & Entry Requirements

  • Biology, Social Sciences, Statistics
  • Mathematics and English proficiency
  • Minimum APS and entrance exam

General Entry Requirements

To study Medicine, students typically need:

  • High scores in Biology, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Strong communication and analytical skills
  • Passion for helping others and ethical responsibility
